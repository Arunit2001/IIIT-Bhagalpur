    IIIT Bhagalpur


    FOUNDATION

    Indian Institute of Information Technology, Bhagalpur (IIIT Bhagalpur) is one of the IIITs set up by MHRD, Govt of India. It started functioning from the academic year 2017. The institute started with B. Tech programmes in two branches namely Computer Science Engineering (60 seats) and Electronics and Communication Engineering (60 seats) in 2017. From 2018, a new branch namely Mechatronics Engineering (30 seats) is being started.

    STUDENT'S LIFE

    The students have started several clubs under the mentorship of faculties and are learning a lot of skills from their seniors. All the students are actively participating in the Technical and cultural activities. Udyatava is the IIITB annual sport fest in which all students actively participates. Students actively attends all the clubs taken by the seniors and they develop their skills.

    Computer Science (CSE)

    B-tech course is offered in this departmet. Dedicated Computer Labs are there for better programming knowledge. This branch intakes of total 60 seats.

    Electronics and Communications Engineering (ECE)

    B-tech course is offered in this departmet. Dedicated VLSI and ECE Labs are there for better understanding of circuit logics and device working. This branch intakes of total 60 seats.

    Mechatronics

    B-tech course is offered in this departmet. Special mechatronics lab is established for workshops loaded with variety of instruments. This is the newest branch in the college and intakes of total 30 seats.
